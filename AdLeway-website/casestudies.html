01 / E-commerce Acceleration

Maximizing Average Order Value (AOV)

We partnered with a prominent online retailer specializing in niche home goods. Their challenge was stagnant Average Order Value (AOV) despite high traffic. Our solution focused on predictive personalization, intelligent cross-selling algorithms integrated with their platform, and highly segmented email marketing campaigns post-checkout. The results were a significant 45% increase in AOV within the first two quarters, improving lifetime customer value and overall profitability.

Key Outcome: $1.2M annual revenue increase from AOV optimization alone. CTR on cross-sells improved by 15%.

02 / SaaS Lead Generation Overhaul

Tripling Marketing Qualified Leads

A B2B SaaS platform needed to generate truly qualified leads. We redesigned their entire funnel, replacing generic e-books with interactive tools and high-value webinars. Supported by automation and lead scoring, the outcome was a tripling of MQL rate and a 20% increase in conversion ratio.

Key Outcome: 3x MQL volume with a 20% cleaner pipeline.

03 / Healthcare Digital Trust & Accessibility

Seamless Patient Experience & Booking

A hospital group faced high bounce rates on appointment pages. We redesigned the booking flow for clarity and accessibility. Post-launch, they achieved a 70% lift in successful patient bookings and a 40% reduction in customer service calls.

Key Outcome: Improved satisfaction and direct appointment conversions.

04 / Financial Compliance & Lead Funnels

Secure, High-Speed Lead Funnels

For a wealth management firm, we built secure, high-speed lead funnels ensuring full GDPR and CCPA compliance. This allowed scalable paid media with zero compliance violations.

Key Outcome: Scalable, risk-free growth with 0% violation rate.

05 / Media Platform Reach Scaling

Optimized Audience Expansion

A new streaming platform saw its subscriber acquisition rate increase by 110% through optimized paid media and dynamic creative testing, while reducing CAC by 18%.

Key Outcome: Doubled subscriber velocity with sustainable costs.
